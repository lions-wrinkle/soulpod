Overview

SoulPods are devices designed to protect our souls in a hostile environment.

Each NFT represents a SoulPod containing one or more souls.

Hosted on the Algorand blockchain for its security, speed, low fees and negative carbon footprint.

The project is a verified collection on nftexplorer, RandGallery and ab2.gallery.

Traits

Models 'A' which can fit 2 or 3 adult souls, 'C' for a single child soul and 'F' for families of 2 to 4 people

Options LiDAR, sound system and levitation system (rare)

Landscapes Sun, Stars, Crescent Moon, Sunset, Mountains, Clouds and Moonrise (rare)

Case colors Black or Silver (rare)

Souls An almost infinite number of souls with different color combinations and animations.

Properties and rarity indexes can be viewed on nftexplorer, thanks to the community-driven arc69 NFT standard.
