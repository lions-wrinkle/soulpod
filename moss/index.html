Overview

Continuing the SoulPod story, SoulPods Moss are devices designed to protect the last fragile living organisms in a hostile environment. These are the only possible living companions for the lonely SoulPods.

Each NFT represents a SoulPod Moss containing a unique moss composition.

Hosted on the Algorand blockchain for its security, speed, low fees and negative carbon footprint.

The project is a verified collection on nftexplorer, RandGallery.

Traits

Each SoulPod Moss matches its related SoulPod with the same model type (size), landscape and case color.

The pods and landscapes has been designed by hand, moss is drawn and animated through a purely generative process.

Properties and rarity indexes can be viewed on nftexplorer, thanks to the community-driven arc69 NFT standard.
